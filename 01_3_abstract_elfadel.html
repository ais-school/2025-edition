TITLE

Topological Data Analytics for AI Pipelines



PRESENTER

Prof. Ibrahim Elfadel, Khalifa University, UAE



DESCRIPTION

In these lectures, I will adopt a data analytics perspective to give an introduction to some of the fundamental concepts of computational algebraic topology, including simplicial complexes, homological algebra, and persistence homology, and how they are used to make sense of the shape of point cloud data. I will give several examples illustrating how these concepts have been successfully applied to problems of practical importance in the fields of networking, logistics, computer vision, and medical imaging, among many others. The lectures will include software demos illustrating how the seemingly abstract concepts of Algebraic Topology have been turned into practical tools for data analytics within standard AI pipelines for Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. Finally, I will highlight some of the promising research directions for integrating Topological Data Analytics within AI hardware acceleration paradigms.



ABOUT THE SPEAKER

Dr. Ibrahim (Abe) M. Elfadel is Professor of Computer and Information Engineering at Khalifa University, Abu Dhabi, UAE. He leads the Secure Intelligent Systems Lab with a broad portfolio of research projects covering various aspects of the hardware and software of secure cyber physical systems. He was most recently recognized with the Khalifa University Service Excellence Award and is the Editor of the newly established Springer book series: "Synthesis Lectures on Circuits and Systems for Artificial Intelligence".
