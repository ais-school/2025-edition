How to Reach Favignana


If you land in Trapani, take a taxi or shuttle to the port and catch a Liberty Lines hydrofoil to Favignana.
If you land in Palermo, first take a bus or train to Trapani, then go to the port and board a Liberty Lines hydrofoil to Favignana.
Check schedules and buy tickets at www.libertylines.it.
