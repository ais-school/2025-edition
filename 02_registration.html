REGISTRATION


Instructions:

  • For registration, please visit our secure registration site. The registration rates and deadlines are listed in the table below.
  • Once you have completed the procedure, you will be sent an email from the University of Verona secretariat (segreteria.dimi@ateneo.univr.it) in 3/4 working days with instructions for completing the payment for the Conference or Summer School registration. The payment will be made through the pagoPa platform.
  • Once you have completed the payment through the pagoPa platform, you will be sent an invoice via email containing all the information provided in the registration phase.
For any registration inconvenient, please send an email to segreteria.dimi@ateneo.univr.it.

If you have any questions about conference registration, please contact the organizers, Prof. Graziano Pravadelli (graziano.pravadelli@univr.it), and Prof. Nicola Bombieri (nicola.bombieri@univr.it).
